About Us

Pharma is not a
side business for us

Established with a vision to serve the pharmaceutical sector with cutting-edge equipment, Guna Steels has emerged as a reliable manufacturer and fabricator of stainless steel pharma-grade machinery and components.

Backed by years of hands-on experience and an expert technical team, we understand the critical hygiene and regulatory requirements of pharma facilities — and we deliver accordingly. Our workshop in Thindivanam, Tamil Nadu is equipped with modern fabrication tools and quality control processes to ensure each product meets strict standards.
